About Me

Aidan McCormick

Built on storytelling.
Driven by craft.

I started McCormick Media in 2019 with one goal: create visuals that actually mean something. Based in Orange County and Santa Barbara, I work directly with businesses, athletes, organizations, and individuals to produce photography and video that stands out.

Over the years I've built relationships across a wide range of industries — from high school athletics to nonprofits to local brands — and every project has sharpened how I approach the work. I'm not just pointing a camera; I'm figuring out what story needs to be told and how to tell it well.

My background includes advanced film coursework and years behind the lens, and that foundation shows in every frame. Whether it's a short film, event coverage, or a brand campaign, I bring the same level of focus and care to the job.

— Aidan M. McCormick, Founder
