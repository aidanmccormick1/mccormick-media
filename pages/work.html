Video Projects

01
Leadership

FIVE

Trusted with leading video production for FIVE, I produced a full promotional campaign for the Men's Leadership Breakfast Groups featuring a dynamic interview and a clean, compelling ad designed to drive sign-ups and spark engagement. From scripting and filming to editing and publishing, I handled every step of the process working closely with the Director and Founder. The goal was to capture and communicate FIVE's core mission: helping men grow in purpose, leadership, and faith through intentional community. The final content was well received, energizing the launch and helping build momentum around the program.

02
Conference

OCL 2024 Recap

We were entrusted with video production for OCLA17.org and the California Association of Activity Directors, tasked with creating a dynamic recap video for one of the most exhilarating leadership conferences for students. Our team of two videographers captured the energy and excitement of the four-day camp, producing a high-energy video shown on the final day in front of thousands of students. The result was a captivating recap that far surpassed the traditional slideshow format — met with overwhelming enthusiasm and praise, and setting a new standard for how these moments are celebrated.

03
Film Festival

DPFF Advertisement

Working closely with the Dana Point Entertainment Arts Council, we produced a compelling promotional advertisement for the Dana Point Film Festival. This video was designed to inspire students to submit their films for the prestigious awards category. The ad was prominently featured on the festival's website, driving a surge in submissions from across the country. This worldwide film festival, renowned for celebrating the unique surf culture of Dana Point, showcases the breathtaking beauty of the surrounding area — and our video captured that spirit entirely.

04
Sports

Dana Hills Football Season Recap

We created an engaging season recap video for the Dana High School Football Booster 12 Man Club, showcasing the most thrilling plays from coverage of seven games. The video highlighted the dedication and hard work of the players, while celebrating the vibrant atmosphere of Friday night lights. Our focus was on presenting Dana Hills Football in the best possible light — capturing the energy and excitement that defines high school football and bringing the season's unforgettable moments to life.

05
Award Winner

PTSA National Student Short Film

A personal project by Aidan McCormick submitted to the PTSA National Student Short Films competition — and it won. This film, centered around themes of gratefulness and excitement for the future, utilizes video and music to craft a deeply engaging and emotional narrative. The project captures the essence of human connection, showing how even simple visuals and sound can tell a powerful story. A testament to the impact of storytelling through film.
